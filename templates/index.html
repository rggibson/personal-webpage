Tweets by @RichardGGibson

Welcome to my web page! My name is Richard Gibson and I live in Victoria, BC, Canada. Currently, I am remotely completing my Ph.D. in Computing Science at the University of Alberta. As a member of the Computer Poker Research Group, the focus of my Ph.D. thesis is in artificial intelligence and computational game theory, specifically in domains involving more than two agents. From 2010 to the present, I have been the main author of Hyperborean3p, a program that plays 3-player limit Texas hold'em. Hyperborean3p won the 3-player events at the 2013 Annual Computer Poker Competition and is arguably the best 3-player poker program in the world.

Before engaging in Computing Science, I studied Mathematics and hold a Master's degree in Math from Simon Fraser University. In general, I am interested in fun and challenging problems where I can utilize both my computing and mathematical expertise. I am particular interested in pursuing:

  • Research in Artificial Intelligence
  • Video Game Development
  • Tech Startups
