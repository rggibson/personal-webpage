Welcome to my web page! My name is Richard Gibson and I am currently completing my Ph.D. in Computing Science at the University of Alberta. I am a member of the Computer Poker Research Group where the focus of my Ph.D. thesis is in artificial intelligence and computational game theory, specifically in domains involving more than two agents. From 2010 to the present, I have been the main author of Hyperborean3p, a program that plays 3-player limit Texas hold'em. Hyperborean3p won the 3-player events at the 2012 Annual Computer Poker Competition and is arguably the best 3-player poker program in the world.

While my current studies are in Computing Science, my academic background is primarily in Mathematics where I hold a Master's degree from Simon Fraser University. In general, I am interested in fun and challenging problems where I can utilize both my computing and mathematical expertise. I am particular interested in pursuing:

  • Research in Artificial Intelligence
  • Video Game Development
  • Tech Startups
